Daniela Ursache

13.05.2023 at 08:45 - 11:15 (in the hotel).

Buy Now for 120€

This workshop is performed parallelly (at the same time) with workshop by Axela Frank (maternity).

"Getting a nice variety of perfect poses is every photographer's goal, but it is not always possible with fussy babies."

  • This workshop is for newborn photographers looking to improve their wrapping techniques and workflow with awake or unsettled babies.
  • Daniela will share with you 5 types of wrapping - Criss Cross Wrap, Bows Wrap, Twist Wrap, Flower Wrap and Butterfly Wrap.
  • We will be working with a real baby and you will have the chance to see how Daniela uses the studio light, how she stuffs her props and how to style each setup to achieve perfect looks.

Please note that Daniela will be answering any questions concerning these 5 types of wrapping, but she will not discuss other posing or editing techniques.

Participants will receive a 25% discount code for Daniela's online editing 1:1 class

Margarita Kashuba - Natural Simple style

12.05.2023 at 09:00 - 11:30 (in the studio, outside the hotel).

Buy Now for 80€

This workshop is performed parallelly (at the same time) with workshops by Jelena Rudi and Vika Ray.

"What to shoot?" and "How to shoot?" in the Natural Simple style.

During the workshop, Margarita will demonstrate how to photograph a family with a newborn baby in all of these situations:

  • the baby alone
  • with the parents
  • with the older children
  • and with the entire family

As a result, even if the newborn is awake, you will know how to shoot in Natural Simple style to ensure success.

Without interfering with the speaker, the participant may take own pictures. The ideal lenses to keep on hand are 50mm or 35mm.
