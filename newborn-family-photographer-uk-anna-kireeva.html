Anna Kireeva

Newborn & Family Photographer, UK

Anna is a professional newborn & family photographer from the UK. Starting her own business back in 2014 after having her first child, she hasn’t looked back since.

Before becoming a mother and starting her journey in photography she used to work as Marketing Executive in a very fast pace but creative industry.

When first starting photography, Anna chose to try different styles, different light set ups until she knew exactly what she loves to capture the most. Which can be described as natural, simplistic and authentic motherhood photography. Whether that is a mother expecting or a family with little children running around - it’s all worth documenting.

Having worked with 100s of families and a UK’s leading baby brand for one of their marketing campaigns, Anna has plenty of experience photographing babies and families under her belt.

My aim is to create timeless memories filled with love, connection and laughter.

Photographers just like you gather in Estonia for the largest, most inspiring and education-focused photography event. You'll be inspired by the photography workshops and challenged to improve your skills; you'll learn new techniques and possibly diversify your style.
