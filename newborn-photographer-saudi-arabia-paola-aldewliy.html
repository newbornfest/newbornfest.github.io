Paola Aldewliy

Newborn Photographer, Saudi Arabia, Eastern Province

Paola Aldewliy is a highly accomplished photographer known for her expertise in capturing newborn babies, sitter babies, and maternity portraits in Saudi Arabia's Eastern Province. She has been actively involved in photography since 2009 and has made significant contributions to the field.

Notably, in 2014, Paola became the first photographer to introduce and teach this specific style of photography in Middle Eastern countries. Her dedication to spreading knowledge and skills in newborn and maternity photography has taken her to several countries, including Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman, the United Arab Emirates, Germany, Ukraine, Kazakhstan.

Her contributions to the photography community have earned her recognition and accolades, including being ranked among the Top 5 Newborn Photographers on the East Side of Saudi Arabia by the magazine topofsaudi.com. She is also a proud member of the National Association of Children's Professional Photographers (NAPCP).

Paola's commitment to excellence is evident in her awards and achievements. She has been honored with awards such as the International Award of Children and Family Photographers (BICFP) in 2015 and the International Rise International Photography Awards in 2018, where she received Silver and Bronze awards in the Newborn category.

Paola continuously refines her professional skills by participating in masterclasses conducted by renowned photographers like Kelly Brown, Erin Elizabeth, Rachel Vanoven, Jade Photography, and others. She has also shared her knowledge and expertise as a speaker at various photography events.
